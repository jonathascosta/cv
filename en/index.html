About Me

I started dabbling in software development at the age of fifteen and instantly knew this was my calling. Fast forward to today, and that initial passion for algorithms and task automation hasn't waned—it's grown stronger.

I've had the chance to be both a developer and an architect, always striving for innovation. My experiences range from coding in cafés to collaborating with international clients. I've worked across different time zones to meet client needs.

I'm a fan of Design Patterns, but it's not just a buzzword to me—it's a lifelong learning commitment. Along the way, I've picked up invaluable lessons from each work experience, each project, and even those coding practices that made me scratch my head.

My aim is simple: to share my years of experience and elevate the skills of those I work with. As for growing up, that's optional. What isn't optional is my ongoing quest for knowledge and improvement in the tech world.
